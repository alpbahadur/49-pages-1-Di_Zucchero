Our Story

A Condado institution where Italian tradition meets Puerto Rican energy

The Vision

Di Zucchero was born from a passion to bring authentic Italian flavors to the heart of San Juan's most vibrant district. Our founders envisioned a space where the warmth of Italian hospitality could flourish in a tropical setting.

The Evolution

What began as an intimate Italian eatery evolved into something more. Recognizing the vibrant nightlife culture of Condado, we expanded upward, creating La Piazza Terrace Club - a seamless extension of our commitment to exceptional experiences.

The Experience

Today, Di Zucchero stands as a Condado landmark - a place where you can begin your evening with an exquisite Italian dinner and dance until dawn in one of San Juan's most exclusive clubs. Two experiences, one unforgettable destination.
